About Us

Buildovo is Jabalpur Based Startup, incubated at Jabalpur Incubation Center, aimed at solving the problem of solving the problem peoples who dream of having their own Dream House, Buildovo is a platform developed with Vision of providing its customers all what they need at their fingertips.

The Founders observed that people while constructing their home go through a number of problems, so need of a platform aroused where you can get all what you need. Thus resulted in Buildovo.
